Schoolsoft Calendar Synchronization

This service will convert your SchoolSoft schedule to the "iCalendar" format,
allowing you to sync it with most popular calendar software on the market today.

Note that while reasonable efforts have been gone through to store your credentials securely, they will still be sent unencrypted over the internet, so don't use a password you use for anything else. You will also need to go through the registration process again if you change your Schoolsoft password. However, Heroku will log the data used to generate your encryption key, there's nothing I can do about that, sorry. While I promise not to use it to retrieve your password, if you don't trust me you'll have to set up your own instance to use this.
